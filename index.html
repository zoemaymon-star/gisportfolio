What I do

GIS and data analytics specialist, Hyattsville MD. M.S. from Clark, B.S. from UMBC, about nine years of work that spans environmental consulting, public health, federal building sciences, and satellite imagery. It sounds scattered until you realize the common thread is spatial data that nobody's cleaned yet.

Most of my projects sit at the intersection of messy data and non-technical audiences. I like that problem. The analysis is rarely the hard part. Figuring out how to present dam safety data to a state agency, or fire trends to a policy team, usually is.

Active Secret clearance. Comfortable in AWS, enterprise GIS stacks, Linux. I write a lot of Python because doing the same thing twice feels like giving up.

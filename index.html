About

I make places
legible through
spatial data.

I'm a GIS analyst and cartographer with a background in geography and urban planning. My work ranges from technical spatial analysis to polished print cartography to web-based interactive tools.

I've worked with conservation organizations, city planning departments, and academic research groups — usually on projects that involve large messy datasets, tight deadlines, and audiences who didn't ask to look at a map but needed to.

When I'm not staring at shapefiles, I'm usually hiking somewhere I've previously only seen from above on a satellite image.
